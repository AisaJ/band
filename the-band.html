H_art The Band

The ‘Uliza Kiatu’ hitmakers have come a long way since they began. They are one of the few bands in Kenya that are approachable and one of the best bands around.

They are organized, diligent and entertaining. Regardless of the fact that bands usually don’t last long, we honestly hope that these bands don’t break up because the work they have done is phenomenal. Here are c bands that will make you proud to be Kenyan.
